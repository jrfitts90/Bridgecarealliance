BridgeCare Alliance

Filling the financial gap for people facing medical crisis — covering care, medication, and housing support when insurance falls short.

Our mission

We provide emergency financial assistance to uninsured, underinsured, or benefit-exhausted individuals to help them access medical care and stable housing during recovery.

Donate Now Apply for Help

Who we help

People without insurance, individuals who've exhausted benefits, or families facing housing instability due to medical emergencies.

How it works

  1. Applicant completes short intake form (link above).
  2. Our review committee confirms details and urgency.
  3. Funds disbursed for eligible medical, medication, or housing costs.

Apply or Partner

To apply for assistance or partner as a referral source, use the "Apply for Help" form or email info@bridgecarealliance.org.
